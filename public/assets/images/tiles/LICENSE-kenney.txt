grass-kenney.png, road-kenney.png, water-kenney.png, mountain-kenney.png and
sand-kenney.png are single 16x16 tiles cropped from the full pack spritesheet
(kenney/roguelikeSheet_transparent.png, tile stride 17px = 16px tile + 1px
margin), at tile grid coordinates (col,row): grass (9,25), road (12,25),
water (15,25), mountain (20,12), sand (7,26).

forest-kenney.png is the grass tile (9,25) composited with the pine-tree
sprite at (16,9), so it reads as a solid impassable tile like the others
rather than a transparent sprite floating over nothing.
