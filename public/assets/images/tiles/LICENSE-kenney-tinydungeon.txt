wall-corner-tl-kenney.png, wall-corner-tr-kenney.png, wall-corner-bl-kenney.png,
wall-corner-br-kenney.png, wall-top-kenney.png, wall-bottom-kenney.png,
wall-edge-l-kenney.png and wall-edge-r-kenney.png are the 8 tiles of this
pack's wall-frame autotile set, cropped from the assembled tilemap
(kenney_tinydungeon/Tilemap/tilemap.png, tile stride 17px = 16px tile + 1px
margin), at tile grid coordinates (col,row): corner-tl (1,0), top (2,0),
corner-tr (3,0), edge-l (1,1), edge-r (3,1), corner-bl (1,2), bottom (2,2),
corner-br (3,2).

MapService.bx's wallAutotileClass() picks between these 8 pieces per-cell at
render time based on which of the cell's 4 orthogonal + 4 diagonal neighbors
are floor vs. wall, so rectangular rooms get real corners/edges instead of a
single repeated wall tile. A wall cell whose neighbors don't match one of
these 8 outer-boundary shapes (i.e. it's buried inside solid wall, or a
T-junction/cross this small frame set has no piece for) falls back to the
plain wall-0x72.png tile.
